What is included in the setup process?

The setup process includes account creation, customization of settings, and integration with your existing systems. Our team will guide you through each step to ensure a smooth transition.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Simply go to your account settings and select the plan that best fits your current needs. Changes will take effect immediately.

Is there a trial period available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for new users. During this period, you can explore all the features and see how our service can benefit your business without any commitment.

What kind of support is available?

We offer 24/7 customer support through various channels including email, chat, and phone. Our support team is dedicated to helping you resolve any issues and answer any questions you may have.

How secure is my data?

We prioritize data security with advanced encryption methods and regular security audits. Your data is stored in secure data centers and we adhere to strict privacy policies to ensure your information is safe.
